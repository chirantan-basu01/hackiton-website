About Us

Timeless memories on the brink of oblivion. The childhood playground, the school recess, pen fighting, all the truants played, and the petty things done. They are but distant memories, and yet they feel like yesterday. This October, we dive into memories of the time when life was less chaotic, rather carefree. When Takeshi’s castle was a perfect end to a day full of careless bliss. The good old days of the past.Bits2Bytes,Bengal Institute of technology, the largest cultural festival in Kolkata, presents yet another mind-boggling edition of its 4-day entertainment. This time as you walk down the lanes of the campus, let’s together orchestrate the times we have lived through.And to help you make another one of those cherished memories, BRCA is ready and waiting for you, this 13th to 16th October. Come and feel the nostalgia. See you at Bits2Bytes!

Our Guests

Anupam Roy

Singer-Songwriter

Anupam Roy is an Indian singer-songwriter from Kolkata, West Bengal. He is best known for his song Amake Amar Moto Thakte Dao.Since then, he has gone on to compose, write lyrics and sing for many Bengali films.

Arijit Singh

Singer

Arijit Singh sings predominantly in Hindi and Bengali, but has also performed in various other Indian languages.Singh is regarded as one of the most versatile and successful singers in the history of Indian music and Hindi cinema. Considered as one of the greatest Indian singer of all times, Singh is noted for his revolutionary role in the Bollywood music.

Sonu Nigam

Playback Singer

Sonu Nigam is an Indian playback singer, composer, live performer, host, and actor He sings predominantly in Hindi and Kannada language films.He is often regarded as a "Modern Rafi" and is now considered as one of the most versatile singers of the Hindi film industry.

KK

Singer

Krishnakumar Kunnath (popularly known as KK, K.K. or Kay Kay) is an Indian singer. KK is noted for his clear and soothing voice; broad vocal range and noteplay. He is considered as one of the most versatile singers of India.
